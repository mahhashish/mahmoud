Menus: Move Menu Items
Purpose: This page lets you move one or more Menu Items to another existing Menu. If you choose to move a Parent Menu Item,  its sub-level menus will be moved too, keeping their Parent-child relationship. 

Check the Menu Item that you want to move and click on the Move icon. A new screen appears, in which you can choose the target Menu.Move to Menu: This list displays all your existing Menus the way they look through the Menu Manager. You may choose to which of them the Menu Item(s) will be moved.Menu Items being moved: This shows a list of the Menu Items being moved.
Usage: n/a
Default State: n/a
Parameters: n/a
Toolbar Icons:
  • Move: Click this icon to move the Menu Items.
  • Cancel: Click this icon to abort the moving process.
