Newsfeed Manager
Purpose:

This page displays a list of the Newsfeeds you may provide to the users of your site.These Newsfeeds may be displayed through a Menu Item link. Alternatively, you may also display Newsfeeds individually through the Custom/User Module.

Display Columns
  • Newsfeed:  Name of the Newsfeed as it appears on your site. If you click on this name, you may edit the related parameters. A padlock icon next to it shows that it is checked-out. To check-in, click the name to edit.
  • Published: This shows whether the Newsfeed is published or unpublished. By clicking on the icon, you may switch its state.
  • Reorder: This shows the order of Newsfeeds in the list. Click the arrow icons to move the item up or down in the list. This ordering may be changed by editing the related Menu Item parameters.
  • Category: This is the category the Newsfeed belongs to. Click to edit.
  • #Articles: This shows the number of Articles to display on your site as defined in the parameters for the Newsfeed.
  • Cache time: This is the time between refreshes as defined in the parameters for the Newsfeed.
Usage: n/a
Default State: n/a
Parameters: n/a
Toolbar Icons:
  • Publish: Click to publish any number of selected items.
  • Unpublish: Click to unpublish any number of selected items.
  • New: Click the 'New' icon to create a new Newsfeed. 
  • Edit: Select the feeds and click the 'Edit' icon to edit the Newsfeed.
  • Delete: Click the 'Delete' icon to delete one or multiple Newfeeds.
