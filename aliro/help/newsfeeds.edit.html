Newsfeeds Manager : Edit a Newsfeed
Purpose:

This page lets you edit a Newsfeed Item.

Details
  • Name: Enter the name of the Newsfeed as you want it to appear on your site.
  • Category: Select a Category for this Newsfeed Item.
  • Link: Enter here the link to the feed (as provided by the source).
  • Number of Articles: Enter the maximum number of Articles to display.
  • Cache Time (in seconds): Enter the time between refreshes, in seconds.
  • Ordering: This is the order the Item will display in the Newsfeed Manager. Defaults to last when created, may be changed when editing.
  • Published: Click the radio button to decide whether this Newsfeed will display or not on your site.
Usage: n/a
Default State: n/a
Parameters:
Toolbar Icons:
  • Save: Click this icon to save the created or modified Newsfeed.
  • Cancel: Cancels any change or creation. When clicking while editing, the item will remain checked-in.
