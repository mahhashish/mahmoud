Menus: Copy Menu Items
Purpose:

This page lets you copy one or more  Menu Items to another existing Menu.  If you choose to copy a Parent Menu Item,  its sub-level menus will be copied too, keeping their Parent-child relationship. Check the Menu Item that you want to copy and click on the copy icon. A new screen appears, in which you can choose the target Menu.

Displayed
  • Copy to Menu: This list displays all your existing Menus the way they look through the Menu Manager. You may choose to which of them the Menu Item(s) will be copied.
  • Menu Items being copied: This shows a list of the Menu Items being copied.
Usage: n/a
Default State: n/a
Parameters: n/a
Toolbar Icons:
  • Copy: Click this icon to copy the Menu Items.
  • Cancel: Click this icon to abort the copying process.
