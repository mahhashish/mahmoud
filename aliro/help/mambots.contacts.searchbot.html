Mambot: Search Contacts
Purpose: Search Contacts mambot, belongs to the search bots family of mambots. These mambots have no visual output. When they are enabled, search is also performed inside the content denoted by their names. When this Mambot is enabled, a search returns results also from the Contacts data.
Usage: n/a
Default State: Published
Parameters: n/a
Toolbar Icons:
  • Save: Save your changes.
  • Apply: Apply your changes to the site without saving.
  • Close: Cancel the present operation without saving.
