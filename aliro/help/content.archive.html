Archive Manager
Purpose:

This page shows a list of all archived Content Items.You may filter the display by Section, Category and/or Author

Display Columns
  • Title: This is the title of the Content Item.
  • Order: This shows the order of the Content Items. Click the arrow icons to move the item up or down in the list.
  • Category: the Category to which the Item belongs. Click to edit the Category.
  • Author: The Author of this Item. Click to edit Author.
  • Date: This is the date the Item was created.
Usage: n/a
Default State: n/a
Parameters: n/a
Toolbar Icons:
  • Unarchive: return the Item to its original location.
  • Trash: Click the 'Trash' icon to put Content Items in the Trash Manager. Items are held in the Trash Manager until Deleted or Restored.
